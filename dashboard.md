## Training Philosophy

This fight camp draws from proven methodologies combined into a cohesive framework:

- **Concurrent Training** (Alex Viada) — Develop strength, power, and endurance simultaneously
- **Energy Systems Development** (Joel Jamieson) — Build aerobic base, then layer anaerobic capacity
- **Strength Without Mass** (Pavel Tsatsouline) — Neural adaptations over hypertrophy to stay in weight class
- **Triphasic Training** (Cal Dietz) — Eccentric → Isometric → Explosive concentric progression
- **Volume Over Intensity for Skills** (Firas Zahabi) — Frequent technical work below injury threshold
- **Functional Movement** (Mike Boyle) — Single-leg emphasis, anti-movement core work
- **Fighter-Specific S&C** (Phil Daru) — Exercises with direct cage carryover
