# Phase 2 — Intensification (Weeks 4-6)

> **Phase:** Transmutation / Peak Volume | **Duration:** April 6 - April 26, 2026
> **Weight Target:** 85 kg → 81 kg (continue ~1.3 kg/week deficit)
> **Training Focus:** Peak training volume, hardest conditioning, increased sparring
> **RPE Progression:** Week 4: 7-8 | Week 5: 8-9 | Week 6: 8-9 (peak)
> **1RMs:** Squat 150 kg | Deadlift 180 kg | Bench 115 kg | OHP 75 kg

---

## Phase 2 Overview

This is the **hardest phase of camp**. Everything escalates: sparring frequency doubles to twice per week, conditioning sessions layer on top of each other, and fight simulation circuits appear regularly. Strength work shifts from progression to **maintenance** — loads stay at 80-85% 1RM with reduced volume so that recovery resources can be directed toward MMA-specific work and sparring.

### Key Changes from Phase 1

| Element | Phase 1 | Phase 2 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Sparring | 1x/week (Thu) | **2x/week (Tue PM + Thu PM)** |
| Strength Goal | Progressive overload | **Maintenance (80-85% 1RM)** |
| Conditioning | Single session days | **Double conditioning on some days** |
| Tempo | 3-1-1-0 (varied) | **3-1-X-0 (explosive concentric throughout)** |
| Rest Periods | Standard (2-3 min) | **Tightened on conditioning work** |
| Accessories | General | **MMA-specific replacements** |
| Fight Simulation | Occasional | **Frequent (2-3x/week)** |
| Opponent Drilling | None | **Integrated into technique sessions** |

### Tempo Key

All strength movements use **3-1-X-0** unless otherwise noted:
- **3** = 3 sec eccentric (lowering)
- **1** = 1 sec pause at bottom
- **X** = eXplosive concentric (as fast as possible)
- **0** = no pause at top

### Intensity Key

| Marker | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| 🟢 | Easy — recovery pace, low CNS demand |
| 🟡 | Moderate — working hard but controlled |
| 🔴 | Hard — high intensity, high CNS demand, full effort |
