# Week 1 — Base Volume

> **Mesocycle:** Base / Accumulation
> **Training Age Context:** Intermediate-Advanced
> **Bodyweight:** 87-90 kg | **Height:** 185 cm
> **Global RPE Target:** 6-7 (leave 3-4 reps in reserve on all strength work)
> **Theme:** Movement quality, baseline establishment, conservative loading
> **1RMs:** Squat 150 kg | Deadlift 180 kg | Bench 115 kg | OHP 75 kg

---

## Monday

### AM — Strength: Upper Push/Pull (60 min) 🟡

**Warm-Up** (10 min) — Reference `warmups.md` for full upper-body protocol

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Load | Rest | Tempo | RPE | Notes |
|----------|-------------|------|------|-------|-----|-------|
| Bench Press | 4 x 4 | 82 kg (71%) | 3 min | 3-1-1-0 | 6-7 | Retract scaps, feet flat, control the eccentric. No bouncing off chest. |
| Weighted Pull-Up | 4 x 4 | BW + 15 kg | 3 min | 3-1-1-0 | 6-7 | Dead hang start, chin over bar, no kipping. Full extension each rep. |
| Landmine Press | 3 x 8 | Moderate | 90 s | 2-0-1-0 | 6 | Staggered stance, press at slight angle. Brace core throughout. |
| DB Row | 3 x 10 | Moderate | 90 s | 2-1-1-0 | 6 | Chest-supported or hand on bench. Pull to hip, squeeze at top. |
| Push-Up Variation (ring or deficit) | 3 x 12-15 | BW | 60 s | 2-0-1-0 | 6 | Full ROM, protract scaps at top. Slow and controlled. |
| Face Pull | 3 x 15 | Light band/cable | 60 s | 2-1-1-0 | 5-6 | External rotate at top, thumbs back. Prehab — do not rush. |
| Band External Rotation | 2 x 15 | Light band | 45 s | 2-1-1-0 | 5 | Elbow pinned to side. Shoulder health work. |
| Pallof Press | 3 x 10/side | Band/cable | 60 s | 2-1-2-0 | 6 | Lock out arms fully, resist rotation. Breathe. |
| Ab Wheel Rollout | 3 x 8 | BW | 60 s | 3-0-1-0 | 6 | From knees. Maintain hollow body, no lumbar extension at bottom. |

**Session Total:** 28 working sets, ~55 min

**Cool-Down** (5 min) — Pec doorway stretch 2x30s/side, banded lat stretch 2x30s/side, thoracic foam roll 60s.

---

### PM — MMA Striking (75 min) 🔴

**Warm-Up** (10 min) — Jump rope 3 min, dynamic stretching, neck circles, shadow boxing light 2 min.

| Block | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Shadow Boxing | 3 x 3 min (1 min rest) | Boxing only. Focus on stance, guard return, footwork. Film one round for coach review. |

**Cool-Down** (5 min) — Static stretching: hip flexors, shoulders, neck. Light shadow boxing 1 min to decompress.

## Thursday

### AM — HIIT / Fight Circuit (35 min) 🔴

**Warm-Up** (8 min) — Easy bike/row 3 min, dynamic stretching, 2 x 10s bike sprints to prime.

**Option A — Assault Bike Intervals**

| Block | Work | Rest | Rounds | Notes |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------|
| Assault Bike Sprints | 30 s max effort | 30 s easy spin | 8 | Seated, hands and feet driving. Track cals or watts per round for baseline. |

**Option B — Rowing Repeats**

| Block | Work | Rest | Rounds | Notes |
|-------|------|------|--------|-------|
| 500 m Row | ~1:45-2:00 pace | 2 min easy | 4 | Consistent pacing across all 4. Record split times for baseline. |

> **Week 1 Note:** Pick one option and stick with it for the block. Record all outputs — these are your baselines. RPE should feel hard but manageable. Don't redline on round 1.

**Cool-Down** (5 min) — Walk 2 min, diaphragmatic breathing 2 min (box breathing 4-4-4-4), light stretching.

---

### PM — MMA Sparring (90 min) 🔴

**Warm-Up** (15 min) — Jump rope 5 min, shadow boxing 3 x 2 min, partner mirror drill 2 min, light technical exchanges 3 min.

| Block | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Technical Sparring | 5 x 5 min (1 min rest) | Boxing only. 70% intensity. **Week 1 priorities:** head movement, jab mechanics, maintaining distance. Do not chase knockdowns. |
| Round Debrief | 5 min | Quick self-assessment after each round. What worked, what leaked. |

> **Week 1 Sparring Rules:**
> - Boxing only (no kicks, no clinch unless coach says otherwise)
> - 70% power cap — focus on timing and placement, not damage
> - Defense first: if you're getting hit clean, slow down and reset
> - Pick ONE thing to work on per round (e.g., round 1 = jab, round 2 = head movement)

**Cool-Down** (10 min) — Neck isometrics 3x10s/direction, static stretching, ice anything that needs it.

## Saturday

### AM — Fight Conditioning / Active Recovery (40 min) 🟡

**Warm-Up** (5 min) — Light jump rope 2 min, dynamic stretching, arm circles, bodyweight squats.

**Fight Simulation Circuit — 3 Rounds**

| Exercise | Duration/Reps | Notes |
|----------|---------------|-------|
| Heavy Bag Work | 60 s | Mix combos — jab-cross-hook, body shots. Stay technical. |
| Sprawls | 8 reps | Snap down, hips to floor, back to stance. |
| Shadow Boxing | 60 s | Footwork emphasis — angles, pivots, level changes. |
| Burpees | 8 reps | Chest to floor, explosive jump. Pace yourself. |
| Shadow Boxing (southpaw) | 60 s | Work the weak side. Jab and move. |

> **Round structure:** Complete all exercises back-to-back = 1 round (~5 min). Rest 2 min between rounds. 3 rounds total.
> **Week 1 intensity:** 70-75%. This is not a max-effort test. Maintain form throughout all 3 rounds. Record how you feel for baseline comparison.

**Cool-Down** (5 min) — Walk 2 min, full-body static stretching, diaphragmatic breathing.

---

### PM — Open Mat / Light Technical (60 min) 🟢

**Warm-Up** (10 min) — Solo movement drills, light flow rolling with a partner.

| Block | Duration | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Technique Exploration | 30 min | Work on anything from the week. Drill weak positions. Ask upper belts for tips. No ego. |
| Flow Rolling | 20 min | 50% intensity. Catch and release — work transitions, don't hold submissions. Move continuously. |

**Cool-Down** (10 min) — Extended static stretching: hamstrings, hip flexors, shoulders, thoracic spine. Foam roll full body. This is your last session before rest day — invest in recovery.

## Sunday — REST 🟢

No structured training. Full recovery day.

**Recovery Checklist:**
- [ ] Sleep 8-9 hours
- [ ] Hydration: 3-4 L water minimum
- [ ] Protein target: 2 g/kg bodyweight (~180 g)
- [ ] Meal prep for Week 1 remaining days
- [ ] Foam roll / light mobility if desired (15-20 min max)
- [ ] Review Week 1 training log — note any pain, fatigue, or technique breakthroughs
- [ ] Mental prep: visualize key techniques from the week

> **Week 1 Recovery Note:** If you're unusually sore or fatigued by Sunday, that's data. The base volume week should feel manageable. If it doesn't, we adjust Week 2 loading down before progressing.
