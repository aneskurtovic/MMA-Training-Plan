# Warm-Up & Cool-Down Protocols

---

## 1. Strength: Upper Body Warm-Up (~10 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration/Reps | Time | Cue |
|-------|----------|---------------|------|-----|
| 1 | Jumping jacks | 30 sec | 0:30 | Light and rhythmic, get blood flowing |
| 2 | Arm circles (forward + backward) | 15 sec each direction | 1:00 | Full range, gradually increase circle size |
| 3 | Shoulder CARs | 5 each direction per arm | 2:00 | Slow, controlled, maximal range — fist tight, irradiate tension |
| 4 | Band pull-aparts | 2 x 15 | 1:30 | Squeeze shoulder blades together, arms straight, chest proud |
| 5 | Push-up walkouts (inchworms) | 5 reps | 1:30 | Slow walk out to full plank, brief hold, walk hands back |
| 6 | Thoracic rotations (quadruped) | 5 each side | 1:30 | Hand behind head, rotate to open chest to ceiling, exhale at top |
| 7 | Light ramp-up set 1 (main lift) | 8 reps @ empty bar / very light | 1:00 | Focus on bar path and setup — no grinding |
| 8 | Light ramp-up set 2 (main lift) | 5 reps @ ~50% working weight | 1:00 | Increase speed slightly, lock in cues |

**Total: ~10 min**

## 2. Strength: Lower Body Warm-Up (~12 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration/Reps | Time | Cue |
|-------|----------|---------------|------|-----|
| 1 | Light jog in place / high knees | 30 sec | 0:30 | Easy pace, wake up the legs |
| 2 | Hip CARs (standing) | 5 each direction per leg | 2:00 | Slow circles, keep pelvis stable, maximize range |
| 3 | 90/90 switches | 8 total (4 each side) | 1:30 | Knees at 90 degrees, sit tall, rotate hips smoothly |
| 4 | Ankle mobility (knee-over-toe rocks) | 10 each side | 1:00 | Heel stays down, drive knee past toes over 2nd/3rd toe |
| 5 | Bodyweight squats | 10 reps | 1:00 | Full depth, chest up, drive knees out over toes |
| 6 | Walking lunges | 8 each leg | 1:30 | Long stride, upright torso, back knee grazes floor |
| 7 | Banded lateral walks | 10 each direction | 1:00 | Band above knees, stay low in half squat, push knees out |
| 8 | Fire hydrants | 10 each side | 1:00 | Quadruped position, keep core braced, lift knee out to side |
| 9 | Light ramp-up set 1 (main lift) | 8 reps @ empty bar | 1:00 | Groove the pattern — focus on brace and depth |
| 10 | Light ramp-up set 2 (main lift) | 5 reps @ ~50% working weight | 1:00 | Controlled descent, strong drive out of the hole |

**Total: ~12 min**

## 3. Strength: Power Day Warm-Up (~11 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration/Reps | Time | Cue |
|-------|----------|---------------|------|-----|
| 1 | Jumping jacks | 30 sec | 0:30 | Brisk pace, full arm extension overhead |
| 2 | High knees | 30 sec | 0:30 | Drive knees to hip height, pump arms |
| 3 | Leg swings (front-to-back) | 10 each leg | 1:00 | Hold wall for balance, keep standing leg straight |
| 4 | Leg swings (lateral) | 10 each leg | 1:00 | Cross body and out, controlled swing |
| 5 | Arm circles + shoulder pass-throughs | 10 each + 10 pass-throughs | 1:00 | Use PVC or band for pass-throughs — wide grip, go slow |
| 6 | Hip CARs | 3 each direction per leg | 1:30 | Smaller set here — just open up the hips |
| 7 | Hang clean technique (empty bar) | 2 x 5 reps | 2:00 | Focus on hip hinge, explosive hip extension, high elbows in catch |
| 8 | Box jump build-up (low box) | 3 reps to low box | 1:00 | Soft landing, full hip extension on takeoff, step down |
| 9 | Box jump build-up (working height) | 3 reps | 1:00 | Stick the landing, quiet feet, absorb through hips |
| 10 | Light ramp-up set (main power lift) | 3 reps @ ~50% | 1:00 | Fast and crisp — prime the nervous system |

**Total: ~11 min**

## 4. MMA Striking Warm-Up (~10 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration/Reps | Time | Cue |
|-------|----------|---------------|------|-----|
| 1 | Jump rope | 3 min | 3:00 | Stay on balls of feet, light bounce, relaxed shoulders |
| 2 | Wrist circles | 15 each direction | 0:30 | Interlace fingers, roll smoothly — protect the wrists |
| 3 | Neck warm-up (nods, rotations, lateral) | 10 each direction | 1:00 | Slow and controlled — never force range, gentle end-range holds |
| 4 | Arm circles + shoulder rolls | 10 each direction | 0:30 | Loosen up the striking chain from shoulder to fist |
| 5 | Dynamic hip openers (leg swings, hip circles) | 8 each leg | 1:00 | Prepare for kicks — front-to-back and lateral swings |
| 6 | Torso rotations (standing) | 10 each side | 0:30 | Feet planted, rotate from thoracic spine, arms relaxed |
| 7 | Shadow boxing — jab-cross only | 1 min | 1:00 | Light and technical, focus on hand return and stance |
| 8 | Shadow boxing — add hooks, uppercuts | 1 min | 1:00 | Stay relaxed, snap punches, keep chin tucked |
| 9 | Shadow boxing — full combos + kicks | 1 min | 1:00 | Put it all together, progressive intensity, move feet |

**Total: ~10 min**

## 5. MMA Grappling Warm-Up (~12 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration/Reps | Time | Cue |
|-------|----------|---------------|------|-----|
| 1 | Light jogging / movement on mats | 3 min | 3:00 | Include shuffles, backpedals, sprawl checks — keep it easy |
| 2 | Neck warm-up (nods, rotations, bridges) | 10 each direction | 1:00 | Gentle — build up bridge height gradually, never force |
| 3 | Hip escapes / shrimps | 5 each side (down and back) | 1:30 | Drive off the bottom foot, create space, keep elbows tight |
| 4 | Technical stand-ups | 5 each side | 1:30 | Post on hand, kick leg through, stand to fighting stance |
| 5 | Granby rolls | 5 each direction | 1:30 | Tuck chin, roll over shoulder, stay compact |
| 6 | Partner pummeling (if available) | 2 min | 2:00 | Fight for underhooks, stay square, heavy hips |
| 7 | Partner arm drags (if available) | 5 each side | 1:30 | Grip wrist and tricep, pull across body, angle off |

*Solo alternative for exercises 6-7: replace with forward/backward rolls (5 each) and shot entries (5 each side).*

**Total: ~12 min**

## 6. Conditioning / Cardio Warm-Up (~8 min)

| Order | Exercise | Duration/Reps | Time | Cue |
|-------|----------|---------------|------|-----|
| 1 | Light movement (walk / easy jog / pedal) | 2 min | 2:00 | Zone 1 effort — conversational pace, loosen up |
| 2 | Leg swings (front-to-back + lateral) | 8 each direction, each leg | 1:30 | Controlled, progressively bigger swings |
| 3 | Bodyweight squats | 10 reps | 0:30 | Full depth, open up hips and ankles |
| 4 | Torso rotations + arm swings | 10 each | 0:30 | Wake up the trunk, stay relaxed |
| 5 | Progressive build-up on modality — easy | 1 min @ ~50% target intensity | 1:00 | Find your rhythm, smooth technique |
| 6 | Progressive build-up — moderate | 1 min @ ~70% target intensity | 1:00 | HR climbing, breathing picks up but controlled |
| 7 | Progressive build-up — near target | 1 min @ ~85-90% target intensity | 1:00 | Approaching working pace, mentally lock in |

**Total: ~8 min**

## 7. Cool-Down Protocol (Universal — After Every Session, ~15-20 min)

### Phase 1: Light Movement (5 min)

| Exercise | Duration | Cue |
|----------|----------|-----|
| Walking (or easy bike / treadmill) | 3 min | Slow pace, bring heart rate down gradually |
| Deep breathing (standing or seated) | 2 min | 4-count inhale through nose, 6-count exhale through mouth |

### Phase 2: Static Stretching (5 min — 30 sec holds each)

| Order | Stretch | Duration | Cue |
|-------|---------|----------|-----|
| 1 | Standing hamstring stretch (each leg) | 30 sec x 2 | Hinge at hips, flat back, feel stretch behind knee |
| 2 | Kneeling hip flexor stretch (each side) | 30 sec x 2 | Squeeze glute on back leg, tall torso, push hips forward |
| 3 | Standing quad stretch (each leg) | 30 sec x 2 | Pull heel to glute, knees together, stand tall |
| 4 | Doorway / post chest stretch (each side) | 30 sec x 2 | Arm at 90 degrees on wall, lean through, open chest |
| 5 | Overhead lat stretch (each side) | 30 sec x 2 | Grab overhead surface, sink hips away, feel side/back stretch |

### Phase 3: Foam Rolling (5-10 min)

| Priority Area | Duration | Cue |
|---------------|----------|-----|
| Quads / hip flexors | 60-90 sec each leg | Slow rolls, pause on tender spots 10-15 sec |
| IT band / outer thigh | 60 sec each leg | Roll from hip to knee, go slow — this one is intense |
| Upper back (thoracic) | 60-90 sec | Arms crossed over chest, roll mid-back, extend over roller |
| Lats | 60 sec each side | Side-lying, roller in armpit area, roll down to mid-rib |
| Glutes (lacrosse ball optional) | 60 sec each side | Sit on roller/ball, cross ankle over knee, target deep glute |
| Calves | 60 sec each leg | Stack legs for pressure, point/flex foot while rolling |

**Total: ~15-20 min**

## 8. Mobility Flow (Recovery Days / Week 4 Wednesday PM, ~25 min)

*Perform as a continuous flow. Breathe deeply throughout — exhale into each stretch. No rushing.*

| Order | Position / Exercise | Duration | Cue |
|-------|---------------------|----------|-----|
| 1 | Cat-cow (quadruped) | 90 sec (slow reps) | Inhale into extension, exhale into flexion — full spinal wave |
| 2 | Thread the needle (each side) | 60 sec per side | Reach under and through, follow hand with eyes, feel thoracic rotation |
| 3 | Open books (side-lying, each side) | 60 sec per side | Knees stacked, top arm opens to ceiling, let gravity pull you open |
| 4 | 90/90 hip stretch — front leg focus (each side) | 90 sec per side | Both knees at 90, sit tall, hinge forward over front shin |
| 5 | 90/90 hip stretch — back leg focus (each side) | 60 sec per side | Same setup, lean back slightly, feel inner thigh / hip of back leg |
| 6 | Pigeon pose (each side) | 90 sec per side | Front shin angled, back leg extended, walk hands forward to deepen |
| 7 | Frog stretch | 90 sec | Knees wide, hips push back, forearms on floor — go easy at first |
| 8 | Shoulder CARs (standing) | 60 sec (3 each direction per arm) | Slow and maximal — own every degree of the circle |
| 9 | Sleeper stretch (each side) | 60 sec per side | Side-lying, bottom arm at 90, gently press hand toward floor for internal rotation |
| 10 | Supine hamstring stretch (each leg) | 60 sec per side | Use strap or towel, keep leg straight, pull gently toward face |
| 11 | Ankle mobility (half-kneeling, each side) | 60 sec per side | Drive knee past toes, heel stays down, feel stretch in calf/ankle |
| 12 | Wrist mobility (quadruped) | 60 sec | Fingers forward, lean in — then fingers back, lean gently — then backs of hands |
| 13 | Seated spinal twist (each side) | 60 sec per side | Cross leg over, twist toward bent knee, press elbow outside knee |
| 14 | Child's pose | 90 sec | Arms extended, sink hips to heels, breathe into lower back |

**Total: ~25 min**

## 9. Neck Strengthening Protocol

Neck strength is critical for concussion prevention in combat sports. A stronger neck decelerates the head more effectively on impact, reducing rotational forces on the brain. Every fighter should treat neck work with the same priority as core work — it is non-negotiable.

---

### Pre-Session Neck Warm-Up (add to Striking & Grappling warm-ups)

Perform this before every striking and grappling session, immediately after the general warm-up.

| Exercise | Protocol | Rest | Cue |
|----------|----------|------|-----|
| Isometric Neck Hold — Forward | 10 sec hold x 2 rounds | 5 sec between directions | Place palm on forehead, push head into hand at ~50% effort. Resist all movement — neck stays neutral. |
| Isometric Neck Hold — Backward | 10 sec hold x 2 rounds | 5 sec between directions | Place palm on back of head, push backward into hand at ~50% effort. Keep chin slightly tucked. |
| Isometric Neck Hold — Left | 10 sec hold x 2 rounds | 5 sec between directions | Place left palm on left temple, push into hand. Keep shoulders level, do not side-bend the trunk. |
| Isometric Neck Hold — Right | 10 sec hold x 2 rounds | 5 sec between directions | Place right palm on right temple, push into hand. Match effort to the left side. |

- **2 rounds** through all 4 directions = approximately 3 minutes total.
- Use light manual resistance from a partner (preferred) or self-applied with your hand.
- Effort should be moderate (50-70%) — this is activation, not max effort.
- If training solo, a resistance band looped around the head works as an alternative.

**Total: ~3 min added to warm-up**

---

### Progressive Neck Harness Work (Friday Power Sessions)

Add this at the end of the Friday AM strength session, after core work and before the cool-down. Use a neck harness with a plate or chain attachment.

| Week | Sets x Reps | Load | Notes |
|------|-------------|------|-------|
| Week 1 | 2 x 15 | 2.5 kg | Learn the movement. Slow, controlled reps. Full ROM flexion and extension only. |
| Week 2 | 3 x 12 | 5 kg | Increase volume and load. Maintain strict control — no jerking. |
| Week 3 | 3 x 10 | 7.5 kg | Peak load for the block. If form degrades, drop back to 5 kg. |
| Week 4 (deload) | 2 x 10 | 5 kg | Reduced volume and load. Maintenance only. |

#### Execution Cues

1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart or sit on a bench with torso upright.
2. Attach the neck harness with the plate hanging in front (for extension) or behind (for flexion).
3. Perform controlled flexion (chin to chest) and extension (look up) through a comfortable range of motion.
4. Each rep should take 3-4 seconds total (2 sec concentric, 2 sec eccentric).
5. **NEVER do neck bridges or rapid rotations under load.** These movements compress cervical discs and carry an unacceptable injury risk for the marginal benefit they provide.

> **Lateral neck work under load (side flexion with a harness) is an advanced progression.** Do not add it until flexion/extension has been trained consistently for at least 8 weeks and you can handle 10 kg comfortably.
